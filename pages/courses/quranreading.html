Quran Reading Course

The Quran is the divine book revealed by Allah (SWT) as a guide for all of humanity. It is written in classical Arabic, and understanding how to read it correctly is the first essential step toward connecting with its timeless message. Our Quran Reading Course is designed to help beginners, children, or anyone who wishes to improve their Quranic reading skills. With step-by-step instruction and personal guidance, students will gain the ability to read the Quran accurately and fluently, following the correct pronunciation and basic Tajweed rules.

Why Quran Reading Course?

Foundational Skill: Reading the Quran is fundamental for every Muslim. It is the gateway to understanding, memorization, and deeper study.
Correct Pronunciation (Tajweed): Mispronouncing even a single letter can change the meaning of a verse. This course ensures students learn the correct articulation.
Spiritual Connection: Being able to read the Quran with fluency builds a deeper spiritual connection and strengthens one’s relationship with Allah.
For All Ages: Whether you're a young student, a revert, or an adult who never had the chance to learn, it’s never too late to start.

What you will learn in this Course?

  • Arabic letters and their proper pronunciation.
  • Short vowels (Harakaat) and long vowels (Madd).
  • Joining letters and forming words.
  • Basic Tajweed rules for correct recitation.
  • Reading Quranic verses independently with fluency.
  • Common reading mistakes and how to avoid them.

Quran Reading Course Outline

  • Module 1: Arabic Alphabet
    • Introduction to Arabic letters
    • Pronunciation and articulation points (Makharij)
  • Module 2: Vowels and Symbols
    • Fatha, Kasra, Dhamma
    • Sukoon, Shaddah, Tanween
    • Madd (elongation) rules
  • Module 3: Word Formation
    • Joining letters
    • Reading simple words
    • Practice exercises
  • Module 4: Basic Tajweed Rules
    • Noon Saakin and Tanween
    • Meem Saakin
    • Qalqalah
    • Rules of Laam and Raa
  • Module 5: Quranic Practice
    • Reading from actual Mushaf
    • Correcting pronunciation
    • Building fluency
    • Supervised reading sessions

Requirements

No prior knowledge of Arabic is needed. A copy of the Quran (Mushaf) or digital access. Consistent internet connection (for online learners). Willingness to practice regularly. A respectful and sincere attitude toward learning the Book of Allah
